Training Schedule

My training schedule is intense, consisting of both pool and dryland workouts. Here’s a breakdown of my weekly routine:

  • Monday: Endurance training - 2-hour swim session (long-distance sets)
  • Tuesday: Sprint training - 1.5-hour swim session (focus on sprints and technique drills)
  • Wednesday: Strength training - 1-hour gym session followed by 1-hour swim session
  • Thursday: Recovery swim - 1-hour easy swim focusing on technique and flexibility
  • Friday: Speed work - 2-hour session focusing on race pace and starts
  • Saturday: Open water swim or competition prep
  • Sunday: Rest day

I also incorporate stretching and mobility work daily to prevent injuries and improve flexibility.
